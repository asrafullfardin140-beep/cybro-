Frequently Asked Questions

Do the voice agents sound like robots?

No. We use the most advanced text-to-speech AI models available (like ElevenLabs). The voices feature natural breathing, inflection, and tone, making them nearly indistinguishable from humans.

Can the AI book appointments directly into my calendar?

Yes. Our agents integrate with Calendly, GoHighLevel, Acuity, and Google Calendar. They dynamically check your availability and schedule clients directly while on the phone or chat.

What happens if the AI doesn't know the answer?

We build fail-safes into every deployment. If complex reasoning fails or the user requests a human, the AI smoothly transfers the call to a live agent along with the context of the conversation.

Is this secure and compliant?

Absolutely. All data is processed securely via encrypted APIs, and we ensure compliance with standard data protection protocols relevant to your specific industry requirements.
